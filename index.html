The Movement Kreative (TMK) is an agency devoted to Creative Movement Direction — representing artists who shape the visual and emotional language of contemporary culture. Founded by Aeil Park and Stephen Galloway, TMK formalizes a discipline that has long influenced fashion, beauty, entertainment, and art.

Operating at the intersection of image-making, filmmaking, and performance, TMK’s directors specialize in storytelling, embodied direction, and energetic composition — translating intention into impact across portraiture, runway, advertising, live performance, and brand expression.
